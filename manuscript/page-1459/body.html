And that the crying of the miserable man should not be heard, they dosed his mouth that the voice might be stopped.

It may be suspected that some partisan of the King's [Darnley's] murder was there. In that torment they held the poor man, till that often he cried for God's sake to dispatch him; for he had as meikle gold in his awin purse as would buy powder enough to shorten his pain.
